In general, the results will be for multiple named scenarios, and so insights as to the variation of things across scenarios are particularly important. We also want to try to explain the reasons behind the variations, but we never guess. Often the provided charts and data will contain information trajectories over the range 2025 ish to 2060 ish for production, energy consumption, and generated emissions for some collection of processes. We need to explain what the main drivers and causal relations are which result in the trajectories. Sometimes we only look at one quantity (e.g., emissions) at the aggregate level to see which sector contributed the most to decarbonization rather than trying to look at the what causes the changes in the trajectories - that is typically left to focused sections.
